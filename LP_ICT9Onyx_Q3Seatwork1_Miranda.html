CHARACTERS

TED MOSBY

Theodore Evelyn Mosby is the main character of this show. He grew up in Shaker Heights, Ohio and is now living in Manhattan with his college roommates Marshall and Lily. In the series, he is on a quest to find his future wife because he was far behind in life as he thought. He is a hopeless romantic, kind and passionate kind of person who has a dorky personality. His dream was to become an Architect in which he did by the end of the show.

ROBIN SCHERBATSKY

Robin Charles Scherbatsky Jr. is one of the love interests in this show. Her hobbies are shooting guns at the firing range, drinking scotch and smoking cigars. She has a love-hate relationship with her father because he raised her as a boy. In the show, Robin dates Ted Mosby and Barney Stinson. Her profession is being a news anchor at their local news studio but ends up being a very well known news anchor further onto the show.

BARNEY STINSON

Barney Stinson is one of Ted's best friend in the show. His life and his job was in secrecy throughout the seasons. He lived an expensive life without his friends knowing what he did for a living. He brings home multiple girls to his apartment every night and wears suits everyday. He says catchphrases like "Awesome" , "Legendary" and whenever asked about his job, all he says is "please..".

LILY ALDRIN

Lily Aldrin is a sweet and positive woman. Her profession is being a kindergarten teacher. She is engaged to Marshall Eriksen and gets married to him later on in the series. Her hobbies are painting, shopping and most of the time snooping on other people's businesses. She loves meddling with Ted's love life and sometimes even plans the breakups between Ted and his current significant other.

MARSHALL ERIKSEN

Marshal Eriksen is Ted's best friend. He grew up really close with his family in St. Cloud, Minnesota. He is engaged to Lily Aldrin and gets married to her later on the series. His Profession is being a law student and becomes a lawyer soon in the series. He is a goofy, polite and positive person, He is the kindest and most thoughtful member of the friend group.
